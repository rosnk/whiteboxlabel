Features

PERSONALIZATION

Each hotel fully personalizes its application to its taste and selects a range of internal and external services to meet its guests’ expectations and desires.

  • Each property can showcase their unique Amenities, Photos, Promotions, and Services.
  • Provide Social Media Links for Easy Access.
  • Quick links for Bookings and Rewards.
  • Built-in Broadcast Messaging and SMS Chat with registered guests.

User Experience

Every WhiteBoxLabel property will be presented in a cohesive familiar fashion. Users will feel comfortable with the use of the application knowing immediately where to find the information they need about the property, activity, or any other aspect of their visit.

  • Easy User Access to: Reservations / Rewards / Room Service & much more.

EASY PLATFORM

WhiteBoxLabel is simple and intuitive with easy-to- read text, visual graphics, and informative icons for all in areas. There are buttons for direct dial, social media, discounts, and email where applicable.

  • Real-Time Administration, Editing, and Promotional Capabilities.
  • Promote and Inform about In-House Events, Features, and Notices.
  • Graphical Analytics on app usage.

LOCAL PARTNERS

Advertisers on WhiteBoxLabel will be a tangible component of every users visit. Both within and without the property, advertisers can promote their unique aspects of business and attract from across a broad range of interests. A truly magnificent marriage of lodging, retail, and experiences!

  • Advertisers near you!
  • Activities, Dining, Retail, and Events!
  • Cross-Promotion Capabilities!

About Us

Our MISSION is to provide clients with an affordable, scalable technology which will allow them to control the delivery of information and services to their guests. We work hard to make our app user friendly and a business mantra for the hospitality industry. Our previous QR Code solution Guideurguest has been completely replaced by WhiteBoxLabel a fully functionally App. This new Digital Experience will allow us to build and update our technologies with the properties, the guests, and outside merchants in mind.

WhiteBoxLabel is a fully engage-able hotel and concierge App built for ease of use whether you are a beginner or a guru with digital technology. We want to be your “One Stop” Travel App. Let us help you move from print to digital or simply develop solid efficiencies of scale.
